Burger recipe /Home

Description:

A burger is a delicious sandwich made with a grilled patty, fresh vegetables, cheese, and flavorful sauces served inside a soft bun.

It is one of the most popular fast foods around the world and can be enjoyed with fries and a cold drink.

Ingredients:

  • Burger buns
  • Beef or chicken patty
  • Cheese slice
  • Lettuce
  • Tomato slices
  • Onion slices
  • Ketchup
  • Mayonnaise

Steps:

  1. Cook the burger patty until fully done.
  2. Toast the burger buns lightly.
  3. Spread ketchup and mayonnaise on the buns.
  4. Add lettuce, tomato, onion, cheese, and the cooked patty.
  5. Cover with the top bun.
  6. Serve immediately with fries or your favorite side dish.
