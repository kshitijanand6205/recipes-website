Description:

A burger is one of the most popular fast-food dishes in the world, made with a juicy grilled beef or chicken patty, fresh vegetables, melted cheese, and flavorful sauces served inside a soft, toasted bun. Every bite offers a perfect balance of savory, creamy, and crunchy textures.

Burgers are highly customizable with toppings such as lettuce, tomatoes, onions, pickles, bacon, and a variety of sauces to suit different tastes. Whether served with crispy fries and a refreshing drink or enjoyed as a quick homemade meal, a burger is a satisfying choice for lunch, dinner, or casual gatherings..

Ingredients:

  • Burger buns
  • Beef or chicken patty
  • Cheese slice
  • Lettuce
  • Tomato slices
  • Onion slices
  • Ketchup
  • Mayonnaise

Steps:

  1. Cook the burger patty until fully done.
  2. Toast the burger buns lightly.
  3. Spread ketchup and mayonnaise on the buns.
  4. Add lettuce, tomato, onion, cheese, and the cooked patty.
  5. Cover with the top bun.
  6. Serve immediately with fries or your favorite side dish.
