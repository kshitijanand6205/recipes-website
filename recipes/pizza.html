Description:

Pizza is a popular Italian dish made with a crispy crust, rich tomato sauce, melted cheese, and a variety of delicious toppings.

It is a easy to customize with your favorite ingredients and is perfect for lunch, dinner, or parties.

Ingredients:

  • Pizza dough
  • Tomato sauce
  • Mozarella cheese
  • Pepperoni
  • Bell peppers
  • Olives
  • Oregano

Steps:

  1. Preheat the oven to 220 degree celcius.
  2. Spread the pizza dough on a baking tray.
  3. Apply tomato sauce evenly over the dough.
  4. Add mozzarella cheese and your favorite toppings.
  5. Bake for 12-15 minutes until the crust is golden.
  6. Slice and serve hot.
