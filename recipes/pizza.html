Description:

Pizza is a popular Italian dish made with a crispy crust, rich tomato sauce, melted mozzarella cheese, and a variety of delicious toppings. It is baked until the cheese becomes golden and bubbly, creating a flavorful meal that is loved all around the world.

Whether you enjoy a classic Margherita, a spicy Pepperoni, or a vegetable-loaded pizza, this dish is easy to customize to your taste. Served hot with garlic bread or a refreshing drink, pizza is a perfect choice for family dinners, parties, or a quick and satisfying meal.

Ingredients:

  • Pizza dough
  • Tomato sauce
  • Mozarella cheese
  • Pepperoni
  • Bell peppers
  • Olives
  • Oregano

Steps:

  1. Preheat the oven to 220 degree celcius.
  2. Spread the pizza dough on a baking tray.
  3. Apply tomato sauce evenly over the dough.
  4. Add mozzarella cheese and your favorite toppings.
  5. Bake for 12-15 minutes until the crust is golden.
  6. Slice and serve hot.
