Lasagna Recipe /Home

Description:

Lasagna is a traditional Italian dish made by layering pasta sheets with rich meat sauce, creamy béchamel sauce, and plenty of melted cheese. It is baked until golden and bubbly, creating a delicious combination of flavors and textures that is loved around the world.

This hearty meal is perfect for the family dinners, special occasions, or whenever you are craving comforting homemade food. Served with garlic bread or a fresh green salad, lasagna is a satisfying dish that is both filling and flavorful.

Ingredients:

  • Lasagna sheets
  • Cheese
  • Tomato sauce
  • Beef
  • Onion

Steps:

  1. Boil the pasta sheets.
  2. Cook the meat.
  3. Add tomato sauce.
  4. Layer pasta and sauce.
  5. Add cheese.
  6. Bake for 40 minutes.
