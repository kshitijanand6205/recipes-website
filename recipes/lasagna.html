Lasagna Recipe /Home

Description:

Lasagna is a classic Italian dish made with pasta, cheese, meat sauce and baked until golden.

It is a delicious, cheesy and perfect for family dinners.

Ingredients:

  • Lasagna sheets
  • Cheese
  • Tomato sauce
  • Beef
  • Onion

Steps:

  1. Boil the pasta sheets.
  2. Cook the meat.
  3. Add tomato sauce.
  4. Layer pasta and sauce.
  5. Add cheese.
  6. Bake for 40 minutes.
